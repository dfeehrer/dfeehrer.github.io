Tüth
The all-in-one dental hygiene station with access to your Twitter account.
Project

Working with 4 other students I created an IoT dental hygiene station at the Hack@WPI 2016 Hackathon.. We though it would be fun to use an internet of things device with a personality to over-engineer a solution to an everyday problem. I wrote the microcontroller firmware to control the actuators and sensors and a simple Processing (Java) script that used the Twitter API, after our original board met an untimely demise.

Tüth has a UV sanitation bay to keep toothbrushes clean, as well as automatic dispensers to dispense the perfect amount of toothpaste and mouthwash, every single time. And the punchline...if you forget to brush your teeth one morning, Tüth will tell the world using YOUR Twitter account --public shaming at it's finest.

Our project won 1st Place Overall and turned me and my friends into full-on hackathon addicts.
